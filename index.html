About USDtz

USDtz (USDT.z) is a next-generation stablecoin designed to maintain a 1:1 peg to the US Dollar through a transparent reserve and decentralized governance. Built on Ethereum with UUPS upgradeability, USDtz ensures long-term stability, security, and community control.

Token Mechanics

Minting: Only authorized minters (multisig) can mint USDtz.

Burning: Permissionless — users can burn anytime.

Governance: 1 USDT.z = 1 vote via Snapshot.

Fee Distribution: 70% to reserve, 20% to stakers, 10% to treasury.

Reserve Architecture

Phase 1: 1:1 USDC

Phase 2: 1.5:1 (USDC + stETH)

Phase 3: 2:1 Dynamic (USDC, stETH, tokenized bonds)

Reserve balances are verified weekly. Full audit by ChainSecurity.
