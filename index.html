About USDtz

USDtz (USDT.z) is a next-generation stablecoin designed to maintain a 1:1 peg to the US Dollar through a transparent reserve and decentralized governance. Built on Ethereum with UUPS upgradeability, USDtz ensures long-term stability, security, and community control.

A[User Deposits USDC] --> B(Treasury Multisig) B --> C{Reserve Wallet} C --> D[Mint USDtz] D --> E[User Receives USDT.z]

Token Mechanics

Minting & Burning

Only authorized minters (multisig) can mint USDtz. Burning is permissionless. Daily mint limit: 1% of supply.

Governance

1 USDT.z = 1 vote. Govern reserve assets, fees, and upgrades via Snapshot.

Fee Distribution

70% → Reserve Fund
20% → Stakers
10% → Treasury

Reserve Architecture

Phased Collateral

Phase 1: 1:1 USDC
Phase 2: 1.5:1 (USDC + stETH)
Phase 3: 2:1 Dynamic
