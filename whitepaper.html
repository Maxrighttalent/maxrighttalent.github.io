USDtz (USDT.z) – Official Whitepaper

Version 1.0 – April 2025

1. Abstract

USDtz (USDT.z) is a next-generation, multi-collateral stablecoin built on Ethereum, designed to maintain a stable 1:1 peg to the US Dollar.

3. Token Mechanics

4. Reserve Architecture

Phased collateral: 1:1 → 1.5:1 → 2:1 over-collateralization.
